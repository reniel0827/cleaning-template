About Us

YEG Carpet Cleaning Services

We started our small business with a simple vision: to provide honest, reliable, and top-quality cleaning services. What began as a venture with friends and colleagues has grown into a trusted name in Edmonton. As our clientele continues to grow, we’re proud to offer a full range of expert cleaning services.

From residential and commercial cleaning to carpet cleaning, deep cleaning, stain removal, and upholstery cleaning – we handle it all with a focus on integrity and customer satisfaction. Our dedicated team works hard to ensure every space we clean is fresh, spotless, and inviting. Whether it’s your home or business, we’re here to provide exceptional service you can rely on.

We believe a clean space isn't just about appearance—it's about comfort, health, and peace of mind.

Kelvin Canlas & Benneth Esguerra Owner-Operator
